Impacts to Manufacturing

Robots improve productivity by performing tasks faster and without fatigue.

Robots reduce human exposure to dangerous tasks and environments.

Precision robotics improve consistency and reduce defects in manufacturing.

Automation reduces waste and long-term production costs by improving efficiency.

Robots minimize downtime by working continuously and requiring predictable maintenance.

Robotics increases the need for skilled technicians in programming and maintenance.
